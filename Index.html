About Cyber Crime


Cyber Crime means crimes committed using computers, mobile phones or the Internet. Examples include hacking, phishing, identity theft, online fraud and cyber bullying.



Types of Cyber Crime


💻 Hacking

Unauthorized access to computers or networks.

🎣 Phishing

Fake emails or websites used to steal passwords and bank details.

💳 Online Fraud

Scams involving online payments or shopping.

😔 Cyber Bullying

Harassing people through social media or online platforms.



Cyber Safety Tips


  • Use strong passwords.
  • Never share OTP or PIN.
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication.
  • Update your software regularly.
  • Don't click unknown links.
  • Use antivirus software.
